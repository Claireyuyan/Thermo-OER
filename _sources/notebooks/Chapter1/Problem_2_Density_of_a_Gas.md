# 1.2 Density of a Gas

## Problem Statement:
A container with a volume of 2 mÂ³ holds 5 kg of nitrogen gas. 
Calculate the density of the gas.

```{code-cell} ipython3
## Solution:

# Given values
m = 5  # Mass of nitrogen in kg
V = 2  # Volume in m^3

# Density calculation
density = m / V

# Output the result
print(f"Density of the nitrogen gas: {density} kg/m^3")
```
